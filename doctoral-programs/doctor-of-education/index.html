The Doctor of Education

The Doctor of Education (EdD) is a two- to five-year advanced professional doctoral programme designed for experienced educators, academic leaders, policymakers, and professionals who already hold a master’s degree in education, teaching, leadership, or a related field. This programme emphasizes educational leadership, curriculum innovation, policy analysis, organisational improvement, and applied research aimed at transforming educational practice. It prepares candidates for senior leadership roles in schools, colleges, universities, government agencies, NGOs, and research institutions, offering a pathway to globally recognised doctoral qualifications with the flexibility and affordability that LAMS is known for.

The Doctor of Education

Who Is This Programme For?

This DBA programme is ideal for:

  • Graduates holding a master’s degree in education, teaching, leadership, or a related discipline.
  • Senior educators seeking to advance into leadership roles in schools, colleges, or universities.
  • Academics or lecturers aiming to deepen their research expertise and career prospects.
  • Education policymakers and administrators looking to drive systemic improvement.
  • Professionals seeking to influence educational strategy, reform, or policymaking at institutional, national, or international levels.

Program Structure

Students complete advanced doctoral-level modules such as:

  • Globalisation and Corporate Governance.
  • Project Developent, Planning and Management.
  • Understanding Learning and Learners.
  • Creating a Conducive Learning Environment.
  • Managing and Leading Digital Education.
  • Leadership and Organisation Transformation.
  • Ethics in Research.
  • Advanced Research Design and Methodologies.
  • Dissertation.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the EdD programme, students will be able to:

  • Demonstrate advanced leadership, strategic decision-making, and organisational change capabilities within educational contexts.
  • Understand national and global educational policies, governance systems, and emerging trends.
  • Develop innovative, research-based solutions to complex challenges in teaching, learning, curriculum, and educational administration.
  • Analyse institutional performance, policy effectiveness, and organisational development needs.
  • Design and conduct applied research that contributes to educational practice, equity, and continuous improvement.
  • Progress toward senior leadership roles or academic careers as research-informed education specialists.

Academic Requirements

Applicants must meet one of the following entry criteria:

  • Master’s degree in Education, Teaching, Leadership, or a related field.
  • International postgraduate degree equivalent to a UK master’s degree.
  • Relevant professional qualifications with substantial educational leadership experience (for mature-entry applicants).

Additional requirements:

  • Official transcripts.
  • Statement of purpose.
  • Updated CV detailing professional and/or leadership experience.
  • References (academic or professional).

English Language Requirements

Students must provide one of the following:

  • IELTS Academic 6.5 (minimum 6.0 in each band).
  • TOEFL iBT 80.
  • PTE Academic 58.
  • LAMS Placement Test.
  • Proof of previous study in English (subject to verification).
